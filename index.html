ELC

ELC is an English Training Provider having the aim to give the best English Training to learners so that they are able to communicate fluently.

Established

Established in 2014, ELC has supported by distinguishes English teaching practitioners and professionals to serve the learners and society based on the needs. ELC provides various programs and has succesfully trained hundreds of students to achieve better English compentence.

Center of excellence

The research and Development Department creates and develops its own teaching materials and programs supported by attractive teaching techniques to make them interested in learning English.

Vision & Mission

Vision

TO COMMIT IN PROVIDING THE BEST ACHIEVEMENT FOR SOCIETY.

Mission

TO CONDUCT THE BEST PROGRAMS TO ACHIEVE THE COMPETENCE LEARNERS.

Program ELC

Program

1. GENERAL ENGLISH PROGRAM

IS THE PROGRAMS FOR CHILDREN, TEENAGERS, ADULTS ADJUSTED AND COMBINED BY SPECIFIC SYLLABUS TO MEET THEIR NEEDS.

2. CONVERSATION PROGRAM

IS THE PROGRAM FOR THOSE WHO NEED IN IMPROVING ENGLISH COMPETENCY THROUGH THE MASTERY OF SPEAKING.

3. ENGLISH FOR SPECIFIC PURPOSE

IELTS, TOEIC AND OTHERS RELATED TO ENGLISH FLUENCY.

All these programs are conducted Inhouse Training and In Company Training through partnership programs such as schools,universities, companies, factories, hospitals, etc.
